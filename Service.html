S&M Security Service Company offers a wide range of services that you can choose from that will suit whatever your need is. We provide security services for residential, commercial, and industrial properties, as well as specialized services; such as event security, VIP protection, and cash logistics. Our team is trained to handle various security challenges, ensuring the safety and peace of mind of our clients.

Our Services

Physical Security Service

Unarmed Security Guards- For general surveillance and access control at residential, commercial, or industrial sites.

Armed Security Guards- For high-risk locations like banks, jewelry stores, VIP protection, and cash logistics.

Event Security- Crowd control, entry screening, and emergency response at concerts, sports events, and festivals.

VIP/Executive Protection (Bodyguards)- Personal security services for high-profile clients or visiting dignitaries.

Specialized Security Services

Loss Prevention Officers- Stationed at retail stores to prevent theft and reduce shrinkage.

Bouncers/Door Supervisors- Provide security at nightclubs, bars, or restaurants; verify IDs and manage intoxicated patrons.

Logistics & Transport

Cash-in-Transit Services Secure transport of cash, valuables, and confidential documents.

Escort Services for Valuable Goods, Security detail for high-value shipments or property transfers.

Consulting and Training

Security Risk Assessment & Audits Site evaluations to identify vulnerabilities and recommend solutions.

Emergency Planning & Evacuation Drills Helping businesses prepare for natural disasters, fires, or attacks.
