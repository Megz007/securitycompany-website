S&M Security Services Company was founded in 2025 with the mission of providing reliable, professional, and client-focused security solutions for events and private functions across Jamaica. Built on the experience and leadership of its founders. A seasoned security professional with years of hands-on work in the protective services sector. The company is committed to delivering peace of mind through well-trained, PSRA-certified security personnel. The company began with a dedicated team of four highly trained officers and a focus on event security, specializing in crowd control, access management, and incident prevention. Our founding principle is simple: “Protect People, Preserve Property, and Prevent Problems.”

From day one, S&M Security Service Company has emphasized:

  • Professionalism in appearance, conduct, and communication
  • Compliance with Jamaica's Private Security Regulation Authority (PSRA) standards
  • Customized services tailored to the unique needs of each event and client

Headquartered located at Shop 42 Oasis Plaza, Spanish Town, St. Catherine. The company aims to expand to other major cities and parishes, offering a broader range of services including; VIP Protection, Unarmed Guard Deployment, and Security Consulting.
