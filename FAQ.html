Frequently Asked Questions

How is your pricing structured for event security services?

+ -

Our event security pricing is based on: Number of guards required, Duration of the event (hourly or flat rate),Size and nature of the event. Typical rates start at JMD $3,500-$5,000 per guard per hour. Discounts are available for multi-day or recurring events.

Do you offer package deals for weddings or private parties?

+ -

Yes. We offer customized packages: Basic Package (2 guards): JMD $40,000 for up to 4 hours. Premium Package (4 guards + supervisor): JMD $95,000 for up to 6 hours. All packages include pre-event walkthrough and post-event reporting.

What is the cost for patrol and mobile response services?

+ -

Mobile patrol starts at JMD $20,000/month for one daily visit. Response-only plans begin at JMD $10,000/month, with call-out charges per incident..

Is there an additional charge for special equipment?

+ -

Yes. If your job requires special tools (metal detectors, body cams, radios, etc.), they may incur an extra charge of JMD $3,000-$15,000 depending on the equipment type and duration.

Do you offer security consultations or site assessments?

+ -

Yes. Site assessments are free with any confirmed booking. Standalone security audits cost JMD $15,000-$25,000, depending on property size..

Is a deposit required to book services?

+ -

Yes. We require a 30% deposit to secure any booking, with the balance due on or before the event or service start date.
